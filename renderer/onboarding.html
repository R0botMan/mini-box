Welcome to MiniBox!

Quick setup for the best experience:

Welcome to MiniBox. You can use Spotify playback controls or play Local Files from a compact interface.
  • Spotify setup: create a Spotify Developer App, then paste your Client ID in MiniBox setup and use the provided redirect URI.
  • You need a Spotify Premium account for playback control.
  • Before using controls in MiniBox, start playback from Spotify on one of your devices first.
  • If Spotify Private Session is enabled, MiniBox may not receive playback data correctly.
  • Prefer local playback? Import folders/files and play directly from the Local Files source.
  • You can switch sources anytime in Settings, and also log out or clear app data there.
Enjoy MiniBox :)
